LTSS RECRUITMENT & CONSULTANCY SERVICES


What is LTSS Recruitment and consultancy Services.

LTSS is a 100% locally owned recruitment firm established on Jan 7, 2025, with a focus on finding top talent for a wide range of industries to meet our clients' hiring needs. LTSS Recruitment & Consultancy Services was founded in accordance with Tanzanian law and the government's plan to reduce unemployment in the country by connecting skilled individuals with job opportunities that match their skills and experience both locally and abroad. Our team of experienced recruiters is dedicated to providing personalised services to both job seekers and employers, ensuring a seamless and successful recruitment process.


Our objectives.

Our main objectives are to provide top-notch recruitment services, build strong connections within the industry, and ensure mutual success for all parties involved. By consistently upholding our values of honesty, integrity, and transparency, we aim to be a trusted partner in the long-term success of our clients and candidates.
